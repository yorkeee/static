About GreenTaro Global Export

GreenTaro Global Export is an agricultural export company based in Malang, East Java, Indonesia. We work directly with local farmers to supply fresh, high-quality taro leaves that meet international standards for food distributors, restaurants, and food manufacturers.

Our Mission: To connect Indonesian agricultural products with global markets while supporting local farmers and maintaining consistent product quality.
